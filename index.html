Sushi

Chef's specialty of the month would be the salmon aburi nigiri. Freshly-sliced salmon is coated with house's spcial mentai sauce, thereafter lightly torched. Thus alternating the chemical structure of the salmon fats to such an extent that when you place it in your mouth, it literally melts in your mouth! A sure-pleaser.
